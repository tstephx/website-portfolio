# Portfolio Feedback Request Template

Use this to get structured feedback from hiring managers, PMs, and trusted contacts.

---

## Short Version (LinkedIn DM / Slack)

> Hey [Name] — I'm putting together my portfolio site and would love 5 minutes of your time for a gut-check before I publish it. I'll send you the link and 3 specific questions. No pressure to be nice — honest feedback is the goal. Would that be ok?

## The 3 Questions (send after they agree)

> Here's my portfolio: [URL]
>
> I'd love your honest reaction on three things:
>
> 1. **30-second scan**: Open the homepage and close it after 30 seconds. What do you remember? What did you skip?
> 2. **One case study**: Pick whichever card grabs you and read the case study. Where did you lose interest or get confused?
> 3. **Hiring lens**: If you saw this portfolio attached to an application for a Senior PM role, what would make you want to interview me? What would give you pause?
>
> Blunt is better than polite. Thanks!

## Follow-Up Questions (if they engage)

- "Was there anything that felt like it was trying too hard?"
- "Did you understand what I actually _do_ from the homepage?"
- "How did the case studies compare to other PM portfolios you've seen?"
- "Was anything confusing about the structure or navigation?"
- "Did the 'About Me' section help or hurt?"

## Who to Ask (priority order)

1. **Hiring managers** who've reviewed PM candidates — they know what gets someone to interview stage
2. **PMs at your target level** — they know what "good" looks like at that tier
3. **Recruiters** — they know what makes them click vs. skip
4. **Non-PM professionals** — they catch jargon and unclear writing that insiders miss

## When to Ask

- After sanitization pass (remove sensitive numbers first)
- Before you invest in design polish — content feedback first, visual feedback second
- Aim for 3-5 reviewers minimum — one person's opinion is noise, patterns across 3+ are signal

## How to Use the Feedback

| If they say...                            | It means...                               | Fix...                                  |
| ----------------------------------------- | ----------------------------------------- | --------------------------------------- |
| "I don't remember anything specific"      | No hook landed in 30 seconds              | Hero section + card headlines           |
| "I got lost in the middle"                | Case study is too long or lacks signposts | Add section anchors, tighten prose      |
| "I didn't understand the technical parts" | Too much insider jargon                   | Simplify or add context sentences       |
| "It looks like a resume, not a portfolio" | Too much listing, not enough narrative    | Show the work, not just the outcomes    |
| "I'd want to know more about X"           | Your strongest hook — double down on it   | Expand that section, maybe lead with it |
